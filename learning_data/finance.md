# Finance Notes

## Risk and return

Investment return should be understood together with risk. A higher expected return usually
requires accepting higher uncertainty, larger drawdowns, or lower liquidity.

Diversification reduces concentration risk by spreading exposure across assets, industries,
regions, or time. Diversification cannot remove market-wide risk.

## Valuation basics

Discounted cash flow valuation estimates value from future cash flows discounted back to
present value. The discount rate should reflect time value, uncertainty, and opportunity cost.

Price-to-earnings ratio compares market price with earnings. It is easy to use, but it can be
misleading when earnings are cyclical, temporarily depressed, or affected by one-time items.
